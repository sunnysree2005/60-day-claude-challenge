## 📸 Setup: How to Add Your Profile Picture

1. Place your desired image inside the project directory (e.g., inside the `Day10/` folder) and name it **`profile.jpg`**.
2. Open `index.html`, locate **Line 104**, and ensure the image source tag matches your path: `<img src="Day10/profile.jpg" ...>`.
3. Once you push the updated code and image to GitHub, your picture will go live automatically!
