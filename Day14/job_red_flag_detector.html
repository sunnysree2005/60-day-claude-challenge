On-call specifics: "Can you walk me through what a typical on-call incident looks like — how often are engineers paged outside business hours, and what's the average time to resolution?" — validates whether 24/7 truly means nights and weekends.
2
Compensation range: "What's the budgeted base salary range for this role, and how is the equity structured in terms of strike price, cliff, and vesting schedule?" — forces disclosure of the missing salary data.

PTO reality check: "How much time off did engineers on this team actually take in the last 12 months? Is there a minimum encouraged?" — exposes whether unlimited PTO is real or performative.

Roadmap stability: "Glassdoor mentions frequent priority shifts. How do you balance investor milestone pressure with protecting engineering throughput and avoiding burnout?" — surfaces leadership's self-awareness on the culture issue.

Scope clarity: "The JD mentions both refactoring legacy systems and shipping new features weekly. How does the team prioritize when those goals conflict, and who makes the final call?" — probes whether management understands the contradiction it created.
