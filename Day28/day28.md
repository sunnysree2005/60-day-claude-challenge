# 🏥 Hospital Admission Readiness Simulator
### *Experience interactive healthcare workflows, administrative check gates, and clinical compliance systems*

Welcome to the **Hospital Admission Readiness Simulator**, an interactive training and operations workspace designed for **Hospital Admission Coordinators**, Case Managers, and Healthcare Administrators. This single-file simulator provides hands-on experience in navigating the complex administrative, financial, and clinical gates required to authorize safe patient admissions, manage denials, and maintain compliance with industry regulations.

---

## 🌟 Key Features

### 1. **Intake Profile & Dynamic Compliance Rules**
- **Dynamic CMS 2-Midnight Rule Warnings:** Selecting **Observation** status triggers an alert stating the regulatory differences in cost-sharing, Skilled Nursing Facility (SNF) eligibility, and the mandatory **Medicare Outpatient Observation Notice (MOON)**.
- **InterQual / Milliman Medical Necessity Thresholds:** Selecting **Acute MI** or **Congestive Heart Failure (CHF)** highlights clinical guidelines required by Utilization Review (UR) to prevent retrospective insurance denials.

### 2. **Dynamic Prior Authorization (PA) Branching Engine**
- **Approved:** Streamlines the workflow.
- **Pending:** Allows the coordinator to execute targeted interventions (Upload Docs, Contact Physician, Follow Up) to convert the status.
- **Denied:** Simulates a realistic administrative appeal flow with an animated 3-second review timer, demonstrating how structured appeals can recover denied authorizations.
- **Hard Block Safeguard:** A denied PA on a high-acuity ICU admission caps maximum readiness to prevent unauthorized clinical flow from administrative work alone.

### 3. **Weighted Scoring & Dynamic Risk Trackers**
The overall readiness score is computed using a weighted healthcare industry logic:
- **PA Status:** 25%
- **Clinical Documentation:** 20%
- **Physician Orders:** 20%
- **Insurance Verification:** 15%
- **Consent Documentation:** 10%
- **Bed Assignment:** 10%

Risk gauges dynamically adjust in real-time, highlighting:
- **Clinical Risk:** Automatically elevated for **Acute MI**, **CHF**, and **ICU** cases.
- **Documentation Risk / Insurance Risk / Bed Risk:** Lowered dynamically as tasks are ticked off.

### 4. **Milestones & Interactive Checklists**
- Watch an active timeline track steps from **PA Review** to **Clinical Assessment** and **Admission Complete**.
- Complete standard tasks (e.g., verifying eligibility, acquiring signed consents, notifying nursing floors) to visually advance the timeline and increase the readiness score.

### 5. **Financial & Operational Benchmarks**
- Unlocks a **Governance Snapshot** at **≥75% Readiness**, displaying industry standards:
  - Typical PA Turnaround: **3–5 Days**
  - CMS Inpatient Denial Rate: **~8–10%**
  - Administrative Rework Cost: **~$11 per transaction** (CAQH benchmark).

### 6. **Final Decision Console**
- **Under 90%:** Warns that admission is **Not Ready** and provides a dynamic list of critical actions remaining.
- **90% and Above:** Activates a full, printable **Authorized Case Summary** confirming safe admission status.

---

## 🛠️ Architecture & Technologies Used

- **HTML5:** Semantic architecture.
- **Tailwind CSS (via CDN):** Elegant, modern healthcare-themed design system featuring a slate-and-teal dark layout to reduce eye strain.
- **Vanilla JavaScript:** Fast, responsive state machine with real-time recalculations, transition timers, and visual timeline progressions without external dependencies.

---

## 📁 Installation & Usage

Since the simulator is structured as a **single-file web application**, it requires zero installation or database configuration:

1. Clone or download the generated file: `hospital_admission_simulator.html`
2. Open the file directly in any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).
3. Fill out the **Admission Coordinator Intake Workspace** form and click **🏥 Analyze Admission Readiness** to start the simulation!

---

## 📈 Learning Objectives

* **Navigate Payer Complexity:** Understand the critical impact of prior authorization and commercial payer regulations on hospital revenue cycle management.
* **Appreciate Clinical Documentation Integrity (CDI):** Recognize how complete physician orders and structured clinical documentation impact medical necessity validation.
* **Understand Regulatory Guardrails:** Differentiate between inpatient criteria and outpatient observation parameters as enforced by Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS).
