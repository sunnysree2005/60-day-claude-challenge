Welcome, Head of Operations

Alerts will flood in from across your global network — ports, factories, warehouses, and carriers. You have 3 minutes to keep the network running. Every decision moves your KPIs. Choose fast, choose well.

  • Resolve alerts before their timer expires or they'll auto-resolve badly.
  • Better actions cost more but protect KPIs and revenue.
  • Ignoring or delaying is sometimes fine — but rarely for critical issues.
  • As time runs low, alerts arrive faster and stack up.

How to Play

  • KPIs — Service Level, Customer Satisfaction, Inventory Health, Transportation Efficiency, Operating Cost, and Revenue Protected all shift based on your choices.
  • Priority colors — Red = Critical, Orange = High, Blue = Medium.
  • Actions — Each alert offers different responses (Expedite, Backup Supplier, Reroute, etc). The optimal action varies by alert type — read the impact line.
  • Ignore / Delay — Low risk short-term, but can backfire with delayed penalties.
  • Score — Built from KPI improvements minus penalties. Grade awarded at the end based on final score.
